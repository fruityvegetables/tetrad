The Grace Journey It is not always about the money. Paying or non-paying jobs – always do what you are doing for God and the rest will fall into place. Last week, I was contacted by an organization called The Grace Journey about an elderly gentleman (Mr.Pete) and his wife who needed our help. Due to some medical misfortunes, they were not able to maintain their property up to Victoria’s codes and the fines were mounting up. With the help of several volunteers from The Grace Journey and the All-Out Junk & Debris Removal team, we were able to get their place cleaned up. Hopefully, the City of Victoria is satisfied. I will continue to check in on and visit with Mr. Pete from time to time to make sure things are going in their favor.
